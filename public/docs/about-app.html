The BikeTag Project was started in 2018 as a way to make playing the game of BikeTag easy for anyone and everyone to play the game of BikeTag.
Since then, we’ve built tools and features that make it simple for people to join in, whether you’re riding solo or playing with friends in your local bike community. The app is designed to help you find active games in your city, see the latest tags, and jump right into the action — no login required.
We’ve kept things lightweight and free so that anyone with a bike and a sense of adventure can play. Whether you're chasing the current tag or just browsing great bike photos from around town, BikeTag is a fun, easy way to explore your city on two wheels.
