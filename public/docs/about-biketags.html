A BikeTag is a photo taken at a public location that becomes the next target in the game of BikeTag — a bicycle-based photo scavenger hunt. Players ride to the spot shown in the current tag, take a matching photo, and post a new one to keep the game going.
Each BikeTag is part of a chain of photos that encourages exploration, creativity, and community through cycling. You can view recent BikeTags on the map, try to find the current one, or start a new game in your own city.
